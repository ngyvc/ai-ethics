
Welcome to our survey on the appreciation of Artificial Intelligence (AI) technology following its usage. As AI becomes increasingly integrated into our daily lives, understanding public perceptions and attitudes towards its applications is crucial. This survey aims to gather insights into how individuals perceive and appreciate AI after interacting with it in various contexts.

Instructions:

Please read each statement carefully.
Indicate your level of agreement with each statement on a 5-point Likert scale, where:
1 corresponds to "Strongly Disagree"
2 corresponds to "Disagree"
3 corresponds to "Neutral"
4 corresponds to "Agree"
5 corresponds to "Strongly Agree"
Your honest responses will contribute valuable information towards understanding the public's perspective on AI technology. Thank you for your participation. Let's begin!
